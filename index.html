About Immersive Golf

Immersive Golf aims to redefine the golf season by delivering competitive, time-efficient, and community-driven simulator leagues—keeping players engaged, improving, and connected all year round.

How It Works

  • 8-week seasons with weekly match play
  • Play at local partner simulator venues
  • Compete solo or as a team
  • 2-hour match time limits (or 1-hour if format allows)
  • No putting — fast and fair scoring
  • Live score tracking and standings (coming soon)
  • Smartphone scoring for referees and players
  • New season formats and prize incentives
